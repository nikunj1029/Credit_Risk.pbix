# Credit_Risk.pbix
Credit risk analytics dashboard assessing customer spending, credit score patterns, loan performance, and default risk using Power Bi

##  Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes customer income, spending patterns, deposits, credit score, loan performance, and risk indicators.  
The purpose is to identify high-risk customers, default risk patterns, and credit behavior insights.

---

##  Dashboard Pages

### **1️ Customer Credit Behaviour Overview**
- Avg Debit Usage  
- Avg Card Spending  
- Monthly Deposit  
- ATM Withdrawal Avg  
- Credit Score Category by Employment  
- Spending by Credit Score Category  
- Online Transfer Frequency  

### **2️ Income & Spending Behavior**
- Estimated Monthly Income  
- Avg Monthly Deposit  
- Avg Card Spending  
- Deposit Behavior by Income Band  
- Debit Card Usage by Age Group  
- Online Activity Behavior  
- Outstanding Debt by Income Band  

### **3️ Risk & Default Insights**
- Total Customers  
- Avg Credit Score  
- Total Loan Amount  
- Default Rate  
- High-Risk Customer Count  
- Risk Distribution  
- Default Insights  
- Loan Application Summary
